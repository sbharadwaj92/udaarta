About Udaarta

Someone rightly said, a lot can happen over coffee! When a group of classmates from different walks of life discovered a student's life once again as part of the Executive General Management (Batch 32), a casual conversation over coffee turns into a big idea called Udaarta. A common passion in giving back to the society, spreading happiness and smiles to the needy leads to Udaarta. We were ably supported by our batch mates at IIMB.

All of us were willing to contribute individually and were keen to get stuff done. At this point in time, it struck to us that we should do something that shall stand the test of times, sustainable and should make us proud for being a part of this noble cause. Lo and behold people took to this idea of creating a trust like a magnet attracting iron fillings and it has been the endeavor of the team to get this going before completing the course.

Udaarta will be involved in activities to save lives for the needy by helping them connect to donors and identifying cases for supporting education for the needy and deserving./p>

Udaarta is growing under the mentorship of eminent educationists and industry leaders.

Vision

We identify and connect people who need financial help and donors who wants to give back to society. Our aim is to make philanthropy a way of Life.

Mission

Our mission is to create an online platform, a one point contact for people needing financial assistance and donors looking for genuine causes/cases to contribute

Develop a 100% transparent online system which will evolve with technology.

Aid the donors in making donation effortlessly and provide necessary information to make the right decision.

Advisors

We are all in it together! Some very eminent personalities from both Academia and Industry are advising and giving us directions at every step of our Journey.
