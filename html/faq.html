FAQ's

What is UDAARTA?

Udaarta is a crowd funding platform to help people who cannot afford medical treatment.

What is the meaning of UDAARTA

Udaarta in Sanskrit means Generosity, we through this intuitive appeal to the society to be generous and help the needy and aim at making philanthropy a way of life.

Is UDAARTA an NGO?

Yes UDAARTA is a NGO. Our objective to create a platform which will bridge two ends of the society through technology and help the patients who need financial assistance and give an opportunity to the affluent to contribute to the society by giving them a 100% transparent and easy platform .

Does 100% of my contribution really go to help the ping patient?

Yes, 100% of your contribution goes to help the patient in their treatment. It is directly transferred to the partner hospital where the treatment is happening.

If 100% of the money goes to the end borrower, how does Udaarta self sustain

Udaarta will reach out to other corporate and foundations to help run the operations of the NGO and also request for a voluntary contribution from the donors.

Will the patient pay back the money

No the money repayment is not expected since the funding happens to people who cannot afford to repay this money. Priority is given to people who are the bread winners of the family. Idea is to not only save one life but to help the whole family by saving his/ her life.

How can I get involved?

You can always contribute to the cause by helping Udaarta by getting more individual donors, spreading the word thru social media and also help by getting sponsor or can also be a volunteer . Volunteer section will update on different opportunities

How can I get in touch with you?

The fastest way to reach us is through Twitter or Face book. Or mail your request to joyofgiving@udaarta.org

Can I see the finances of UDAARTA

How do we know the patient actually deserve a funding?

The team at Udaarta has designed a due diligence policy to fund any person. Our panellist and the volunteers, who will go thru the case and endorse before we take it on the site, do the due diligence.

How do I know the treatment will be a success?

We have tied up with the hospitals which are equipped with the best doctors and completely trust the ability to handle the case.

Who is the Hospital Partner and how do they qualify to be a partner

Our partners are reputed hospital which are multispecialty and capable of any treatment and in the most economical way.

How do I know my money will go to fund the treatment I choose?

All transaction in Udaarta is available for the public to see.

How do the donation logistics work?

Your donation first reaches the UDAARTA account, the accounts department will tag the fund to the patient it is suppose to go to and then will be updated on the website. The fund is then transferred to the hospital directly where the treatment is taking place.

What all option I have, in case I want to donate more.

You can donate more patient. You can donate for running of the organization.There are only options we have right now. If you still wish to fund only for the treatment and want to leave it to the discretion of Udaarta there is something called as ANGEL fund.

What do the promoters get out of it

The promoters of Udaarta are senior executives with reputed organization and do not expect any money from the NGO. This is a service they are doing for the society.

How do I know my financial information is protected?

Udaarta's online platform (under development) is highly secured and is built using industry standard security protocols. Any financial transactions carried out on the platform is secured using 256 bit AES SSL encryption

Is my donation tax deductible?

Yes donations' are 100% tax-deductible u/s 80G of the IT Act 1961. We are in the process of getting the registration from the income Tax dept.
